Summer of Change

Confidence

  • Keep my mind busy and be confident.
  • Take opportunities without mulling it over and allowing anxiety to win.
  • When someone talks, I can say what comes to my mind.
  • I'm goofy and speak a little formally, and that's perfectly fine.
  • My inner/outer voice is nonbinary. If tired and don't feel like talking, just talk with a swallowed voice, like MoistCritikal.
  • I'm really quite a happy person when I don't let my anxious thoughts consume me.
  • I have to be the one to laugh longer in conversations.
  • People judge out of necessity.
  • Remember how calm I was playing games on the Seagrove vacations? I want that to be how I feel around others.
  • When socializing, always think "just say one more thought" if I don't know what to say.

Nutrition and Hygiene

  • Drink at least 100 ounces of water a day.
  • Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.
  • When eating out, set aside half to take home. When eating at home, use a smaller plate.
  • Half my plate should be fruits/vegetables, and the other should be a whole grain and a lean protein.
  • To make vegetables taste better: sautee, season, or add some dressing.
  • Shower and brush my teeth twice a day, after breakfast and lunch.
  • Take daily multivitamin and Flonase every day.

Community

  • Don't be afraid to ask for help.
  • Show that you are appreciative when hanging out with others.
  • Instead of trying to make others like us, focus on trying to make others like being around us.
  • In group conversations, say things with the motivation of adding to the conversation. Create a good conversation together instead of being the best one in it.
  • Some good universal questions when meeting someone are "where are you from", "what do you do", and "what do you like most about that".
  • Ask questions that start with W or H. "Who, What, Where, When, Why, How".
  • Small talk has a purpose. It is how strangers "warm up" and figure out if they are compatible.

Workout

10:30 PM - 12:00 PM
  • Sunday (Legs 1), Monday (Push 1), Tuesday (Pull 1), Wednesday (Legs 2), Thursday (Push 2), Friday (Pull 2).
  • Have 15-30 minutes of cardio (biking, stairmaster) after each workout.
  • Eat a banana (or some other form of carb) before a workout for more energy.
  • Keep to a consistent routine. Missing one workout will negatively affect my workouts for the rest of the week.
  • Listen to energetic music during the workout and relaxing music afterwards to begin recovery.
  • Working out will give you a lot of self-esteem and confidence. Also, when tired during reps, do "just one more" (over and over).
  • Home workout:
    Repeat four times, once for each exercise (pushups, burpees, squats, lunges): Perform as many reps as possible for 20 seconds, rest for 10 seconds and repeat for a total of four minutes. Rest one minute.

Piano and Singing

1:30 PM - 2:00 PM
  • Singing: Make sure you feel a hum at the front of your nose to make sure you're being forward.
  • Singing: Air and energy are the two biggest factors.
  • Singing: Imagine the pitch in your head before you sing it.
  • Singing: Imagine you are going to sing the song in falsetto, then switch at the last second (helps with pitch).
  • Singing: Inflate stomach and push against abs while singing.
  • Piano: Break down any new arrangements into small pieces that you can practice, similar to how SimplyPiano teaches you.
