Community

  • Don't be afraid to ask for help
  • Show that you are appreciative when hanging out with others
  • Instead of trying to make others like us, focus on trying to make others like being around us
  • In group conversations, say things with the motivation of adding to the conversation. Create a good conversation together instead of being the best one in it

  • Small talk has a purpose. It is how strangers "warm up" and figure out if they are compatible
